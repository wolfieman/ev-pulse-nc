## Length Requirements

The portal includes both a 20-page preparation guideline and a statement that
there is no maximum proceedings length. The chair clarified that there is no
page-count or word-count limit. Treat the 20-page language as a strong
readability/preparation target unless the chair or portal later says it is a
hard cap.

Portal wording captured from Exordo:

- "Prepare the paper for written understanding with length no longer than
  twenty (20) single-spaced pages including tables, figures, and a list of
  references or bibliography."
- "For a regular paper, no fewer than 2,000 words."
- "There is no limit to the maximum length of the paper for the SEINFORMS
  proceedings."

Practical decision for EV Pulse NC:

- Minimum: at least 2,000 words.
- Maximum: no hard page or word limit, per chair clarification.
- Target: condense the 109-page capstone into a readable conference paper,
  preferably near the portal's 20-page preparation guidance if feasible.
- Counted material under the portal guidance: tables, figures, and references
  are included. Appendices should also be assumed to count because appendices
  precede references and no separate exclusion is stated.

## Required Format

- File format: PDF.
- Font: 11-point Times New Roman or similar.
- Layout: single column.
- Margins: 1 inch on top, bottom, and sides.
- Spacing: single-space the body; justify text.
- Paragraphs: flush left; no paragraph indent.
- Paragraph/heading spacing: skip one line between paragraphs and before and
  after headings.
- Title: bold, all capital letters, centered at the top of the first page;
  single-spaced if multi-line; skip one line after title.
- First-level headings: bold, centered, all capital letters.
- Second-level headings: bold, flush left, mixed case.
- Abstract inside paper: introduce the paper with an abstract of approximately
  100-200 words, beginning with `ABSTRACT`; 11-point, single-spaced, regular
  type, justified.
- Figures/tables: place near their citations.
- Equations: allowed; follow normal numbered-equation formatting if needed.
- Appendices: if used, place before references.
- References: use first-level heading `REFERENCES`.

No downloadable Word or LaTeX template was found in the portal. The portal
references Microsoft Word styles such as Heading 1 and Heading 3, but no
template file is linked.

## Citation And Reference Style

The portal prefers bracketed numeric citations, but author-year citations are
also acceptable.

- Preferred in-text style: bracketed numbers, such as `[1]` or `[2, p. 188]`.
- Acceptable alternative: author-year citations.
- Reference list: alphabetical, identified with bracketed numbers.

Example reference formats in the portal include books and journal articles
under the `REFERENCES` heading.

## Double-Blind Rules

The review PDF and uploaded file name must be blind.

Portal wording captured from Exordo:

- "Please DO NOT include author names and affiliations in the PDF file or file
  name. This ensures the double-blind nature of the review process. If your
  study is accepted for publication in the conference proceedings, you will be
  asked to add author names to the manuscript at a later time."

Not found in the portal:

- Self-citation rules.
- Acknowledgement or funding-statement policy.

Practical decision for EV Pulse NC:

- Remove author name, affiliation, advisor, course/capstone context, repository
  references, acknowledgements, and other identifying statements from the review
  PDF.
- Keep any unavoidable self-citations neutral and non-identifying if they are
  needed.
- Ensure the PDF file name does not contain the author's name.
