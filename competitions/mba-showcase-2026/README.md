## What was presented

A 14-slide, 15-minute (+ Q&A) presentation of the EV Pulse NC framework: a five-phase analytics pipeline (predictive validation, infrastructure inventory, ZIP-level equity, workplace-charging demand, Justice40 overlay) producing an audit-ready county ranking for North Carolina's $109M federal NEVI allocation.

- **Top three counties** — Union, Mecklenburg, Guilford — stable across all sensitivity tests.
- **Headline findings:** 58% of NC ZIP codes have zero charging stations; infrastructure inequality is extreme (Gini 0.805), and **84.5% of it is *within* counties, not between them** — so statewide averages hide the real gaps.
