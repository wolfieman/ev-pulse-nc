# MBA Project Showcase 2026 — 2nd Place

**Result:** 🥈 **2nd place** — awarded 2026-05-06.
**Event:** MBA Project Showcase / Business Research Symposium, Fayetteville State University.
**Project:** *EV Pulse NC: A Data-Driven Framework for Prioritizing North Carolina's $109M NEVI Infrastructure Investment.*
**Presenter:** Wolfgang Sanyer — MBA, Business Analytics (FSU).
**Origin:** BIDA 670 — Advanced Analytics (FSU, Spring 2026); advisor Dr. Majed Al-Ghandour.

---

## What was presented

A 14-slide, 15-minute (+ Q&A) presentation of the EV Pulse NC framework: a five-phase
analytics pipeline (predictive validation, infrastructure inventory, ZIP-level equity,
workplace-charging demand, Justice40 overlay) producing an audit-ready county ranking
for North Carolina's $109M federal NEVI allocation.

- **Top three counties** — Union, Mecklenburg, Guilford — stable across all sensitivity tests.
- **Headline findings:** infrastructure inequality is extreme (Gini 0.805), and
  **84.5% of it is within counties, not between them** — so statewide averages hide the
  real gaps.
